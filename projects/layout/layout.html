What is the Jurassic Park?

Jurassic Park is a science fiction movie, directed by Steven Spielberg. This movie is based on a novel written by Michael Crichton. The main theme is genetic engineering revives dinosaurs. The theme points out the ethic problem caused by human scientific technology. The line “Life Finds a Way” refers to how powerful nature is.

Inside the Jurassic Park, massive electric fences and gates are installed to contain the dinosaurs. The tourists experience amazing scenery of watching live dinosaurs by taking special cars. However, due to the limitations of technology and human greed, the dinosaurs escaped and the island fell into chaos. With its groundbreaking CGI and the theme, this movie remains a masterpiece.
