Overview of Korean Seasonal Festivals

Festivals summary
Festival Name Season Location Month Key highlights Recommended for
Cherry Blossom Festival Spring Jinhae, Gyeongsangnam-do March - April Cherry Blossom, Military Band Parade Couples, Families
Canola Flower Festival Seogwipo-si, Jeju-do Yellow Canola Flowers, Flower Trail Walk Families, Nature lovers
Mud Festival Summer Dacheon Beach, Boryeong July - August Mud Experience Program Friends, Adventure Seekers
Sea Festival Haeundae / Gwangalli, Busan August Beach Parties, Concerts Young Adults, Beach Lovers
International Mask Dance Festival Autumn Andong, Gyeongsangbuk-do September - October Traditional Folk Art, Street Parade Students, Seniors
Lantern Festival Jinju, Gyeongsangnam-do October Floating lanterns, history Couples, Photographers
Sancheoneo Ice Festival Winter Hwacheon, Gangwon-do January - February Ice Fishing, Enjoying Fish Raw Families, Adventure Seekers
Taebaeksan Snow Festival Taebaek, Gangwon-do Snow Sculpture Exhibition, Winter Activities Families, Nature Lovers
