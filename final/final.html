Interesting things about Korean Festivals

  • My family has 12 members (excluding spouses). Because our family is quite large in Korea, we were featured on a Korean TV program.
  • Among the ten siblings, two of us are married, including me. We are the only ones who live in the United States.
  • After all their children became adults, my parents began serving as couple missionaries for our church.

Overview

The fifth older brother starts his career as a nurse

My fifth older brother, who graduated from a university with a degree and license in nursing, was hired from a hospital recently. He works in the intensive care unit (ICU), and with his kindness and patience, we believe he'll do great. His words, “I find a sense of accomplishment when I take care of the people in need,” have become example to me.

Nephews and niece: Halloween and birthday celebration

My nephews and niece attended a Halloween party, wearing costumes. They dressed up as Harry Potter, Simba and Nala. One special thing is that my oldest nephew's birthday is the day before Halloween. We hope they have a wonderful time with all the celebrations!

Me and my husband: Visiting Korea this winter

My husband, Hyeongjin, and I, who are studying abroad, are going to visit Korea! We feel that seeing our beloved family is such a blessing. Even though the weather will be cold this winter in Korea, our hearts will be warm.
