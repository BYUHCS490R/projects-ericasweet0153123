Gender
  • The attribute action used in the form tag specifies the destination of the data entered by the user.
  • The attribute used in the form tag defines how to send the data (e.g., using GET or POST).
  • GET is used for simple requests such as searches. With GET, the data appears in the URL. POST is used to send sensitive information such as login details. The data does not appear in the URL.
